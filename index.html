ABOUT ME

A great DJ with and a good sense of timing and co-ordination, able to lead the crowd to the dance floor. Able to produce tracks, remix tracks, make bootlegs & mashups. Looking for a job/an internship to get more experience DJ’ing in clubs and hopefully be able to create my own event organization and play in clubs around London and out of London in other various countries & clubs. Having a good sense of timing and co-ordination came from being able to play drums, which is still ongoing and has been for 7 years. Worked as mobile DJ for various events mainly at high school proms and clubs around Jakarta, Indonesia. Been DJ’ing for 3 years & producing for 4 years still progressing in both areas as time goes by and open to different kinds of music. Able to take song requests from the crowd depending on whether it suits or goes well into my playlist or not. Willing to ask other people’s opinions on what songs they like best by giving them 2 tracks to choose from.
